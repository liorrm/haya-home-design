About

I don’t follow trends, I create them.

The art of designing the perfect dream home is my passion. I have designed residential and commercial projects around the world, each of which is uniquely tailored to the taste, budget, and needs of my clients.

My career began in Israel where I founded what has become a thriving design firm for over 20 years. My business expanded to London where I provided interior and architectural design for commercial and residential projects. I have lived in Los Angeles for the past 15 years where I have been a top Real Estate Specialist for Barnes International Luxury Real Estate and have designed numerous projects, small and large, from single family residences to small commercial shopping centers throughout California.

Wearing the two hats of real estate agent and building designer, I will guide you through every step of your project, from finding your next house to making it your dream home.

My commitment to you is the capability to understand your goals, fulfill your needs, and turn your dreams into reality.
