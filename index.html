About

I don’t follow trends, I create them.

The art of design is my passion. I have designed residential and commercial projects around the world, each of which was uniquely tailored to the style, budget, and needs of my clients.

My career began in Israel where I founded what has become a thriving design firm for over 30 years. My business expanded to London where I provided interior and architectural design for commercial and residential projects. I have lived in Los Angeles since 2000 and have designed numerous of architectural projects, small to large, from single family residences to small commercial shopping centers throughout California.

In the last couple of years I started to help people to legalize their unpermitted construction work, and became very knowledgeable in this field.
